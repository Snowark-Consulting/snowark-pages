# Not Forgotten Systems — Customers & Case Studies

> See [[Business Overview]] (knowledge/business.md) for company context and [[Services]] (knowledge/services.md) for what customers buy.

## Target Customer Profile
Service business owners who:
- Send quotes regularly
- Struggle with manual follow-up
- Lose revenue from forgotten/unresponded quotes
- Want automation without complex setup

## Case Study: Travis Krieg Painting
- Recovered $59,000 in jobs within three weeks
- Included work from months earlier that had gone cold
- Used automated email + SMS follow-up sequences

## Key Industries
- Painting (Travis Krieg)
- Trades (electrical, plumbing, HVAC, construction)
- Home services (cleaning, landscaping, pest control)
- Professional services (consulting, accounting, legal)
