## Not Forgotten (SaaS Platform)
A quote follow-up automation platform for service businesses.

### Key Capabilities
- Monitors email for quote messages (CC/BCC)
- Automated follow-up sequences (email + SMS)
- Response detection and owner notification
- Reporting and revenue recovery tracking

### Technical Notes
- Currently a web-based SaaS platform
- Website at notforgotten.com.au
- No API integration required — works via email CC/BCC
- SMS integration included

### Competitors
Automated quote follow-up is a growing category. Direct competitors may include CRM platforms with follow-up modules (HubSpot, Pipedrive), dedicated follow-up tools, and manual VA services.
