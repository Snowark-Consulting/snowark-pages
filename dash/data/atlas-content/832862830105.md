# Not Forgotten Systems

## Overview
Not Forgotten Systems is a SaaS platform that automates quote follow-up for service businesses. Customers CC or BCC Not Forgotten on their quote emails, and the system handles follow-up by email and SMS automatically until the prospect responds. See [[Products & Services]] (knowledge/products.md) and [[Services]] (knowledge/services.md) for what's sold.

## Product: Not Forgotten

### How it works
1. Customer sends a quote as normal, CC/BCC Not Forgotten
2. System runs automated email + SMS follow-up sequences
3. Customer responses are tracked; the business owner is notified
