The truth

You built something real. But somewhere along the way, the admin took over. The tools that were meant to help now demand your attention. The data that should guide your decisions is scattered across platforms. Every hour spent in the weeds is an hour not spent on growth.

The problem

Four ways it quietly compounds

01

Fragmented data

Your CRM, project tools, spreadsheets, and inbox do not talk to each other. Every decision rests on a hunch rather than a single source of truth.

02

Admin eating your afternoons

Email follow-ups, data entry, quoting, reporting. The work that keeps the business running but keeps you from building it.

03

Things falling through cracks

Leads go cold. Quotes sit unapproved. Clients wait for updates. No one has a complete view of what needs attention right now.

04

Competitors pulling ahead

While you are working in the business, others are adopting AI to reduce costs, close more deals, and move faster. The gap is widening. You can feel it.

How we work

Business strategy first, AI second

Most AI vendors start with the tool and look for a problem. We start with the P&L. We find the bottleneck, then point the AI at it.

02 Configure
03 Run
01

Map your operations

We learn your workflows, your tools, and where the friction lives. We identify the single biggest constraint on your growth: awareness, conversion, or operations. We research your stack before we even meet.

02

Configure your agent

Using pre-built infrastructure, we deploy an agent that plugs into your existing tools and starts working at the highest-leverage point. Not a generic chatbot. A configured business partner that understands your context and acts on it.
